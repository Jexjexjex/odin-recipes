Delicious Salmon Teriyaki Style

Allrecipes

Are you ready to discover how to create the best Crispy Chicken out there?

The following contains the required ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Teriyaki Sauce of any brand
  • Sesame Oil and seeds
  • Fish Knife is recommended
  • Seasoning (optional)

The following kitchen tools are recommended:

  • Oven
  • Baking Sheet

Here are the steps to create the best salmon you have ever tasted:

  1. Slice the Salmon vertically to ensure that the meat fully cooks in the inside
  2. Place the fish on a baking sheet
  3. Put your seasoning on both sides of the fillet
  4. Brush your Teriayki sauce on both sides of the fillet. Apply generously
  5. Put the Salmon into the oven, set the degrees in F to 400o, and set a timer to about 19 minutes

And Viola! Enjoy!
