Salvating Curry Japanese Style

Allrecipes

Are you ready to discover how to create the best Crispy Chicken out there?

The following contains the required ingredients:

  • Curry Cubes
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 5+ carrots
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 5 pounds of stew meat
  • 5 cups of water (depends on pot)
  • salt or soy sauce for additional saltiness (optional)

The following kitchen tools are recommended:

  • Big Pot
  • medium pan
  • Potato Peeler or pairing knife
  • laddle for stirring and taste
  • cutting board

Here are the steps to create the best curry you have ever tasted:

  1. Cut the stew meat to bite size pieces and fry the meat up in the medium pan until it looks white/brownish
  2. Peel the skin off both the potatoes and the carrots
  3. Chop them into bite size pieces
  4. Dice both onions
  5. Through the onions in the big pot first and fry until they are caramelized
  6. When the onions are ready, throw the rest of the vegetables, mix, and wait for about 3 minutes for them to cook using medium setting
  7. When time is up, throw the meat in the pan along with the water, raise the temperature to high, and wait until the water starts boiling
  8. Once water is boiling, set the temperature down to medium/low and simmer for about 45 minutes
  9. When time is up, stop the fire, put two Japanese Cubes in the pot, start up the fire back to medium/low, and stir constantly for 5 minutes

And Viola! Enjoy!
