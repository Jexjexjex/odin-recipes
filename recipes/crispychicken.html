Crispy Chicken

Allrecipes

Are you ready to discover how to create the best Crispy Chicken out there?

The following contains the required ingredients:

  • Chicken Tenders
  • 5 Organic Eggs
  • 3 pounds of white flour
  • Bread Crumbs

The following kitchen tools are recommended:

  • Air Fryer (or oven)
  • Strainer
  • At least three medium to large bowls
  • Baking Paper

Here are the steps to create the best chicken you have ever tasted:

  1. Crack the eggs and mix them until they look scrambled
  2. Place the eggs in one of the medium to large bowls
  3. Pour the flour in one of the medium to large bowls
  4. Pour the Bread Crumbs in one of the medium to large bowls
  5. Dunk the Chicken Tenders in the egg bowl, then the flour bowl, and lastly the bread crumbs bowl
  6. Put the dunked tenders on the Baking Paper
  7. Place the tenders in the Air Fryer for approximately 20 minutes on high power

And Viola! Enjoy!
